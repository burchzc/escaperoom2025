ESCAPE ROOM INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to ORNL's Escape Room 2025: The Inside Threat! Please keep the following rules in mind as you play the game.

  • Anything marked by a red sticker is not a part of the game. Please do not touch!
  • Use only "finger strength" to interact with objects. Nothing needs to be forced open!
  • DO NOT mess with outlets, lights, or other such fixtures. There is nothing in the walls!
  • Please be careful with our props - do not throw, punch, smack, suplex or otherwise destroy them.
  • Locks and keys will be used only once!
  • If you need a hint, have everyone raise their hand and say "give us a cheat code!" - we will guide you on your current task.
  • If you have issues with the rubber ducky, remove it and plug it back in.
  • After the game is over, check your pockets to make sure you have not accidentally taken a key, lock, or prop!
  • Good luck and have fun!
